About Lollipop Early Learning Daycare

"I Love Children"

Lollipop Early Learning is a privately owned and operated child care devoted to high quality child care in a safe, gentle, and nurturing environment. We are dedicated to fostering children's growth and development through play. Lollipop Early Learning provides a positive learning environment for infants as young as 6 weeks up to school aged children. We are a cozy home away from home where learning blooms and where children become a part of our family! We help children to explore the world around them, we teach children to be independent, to develop social skills, and to love learning. We believe that all children are capable, independent, and curious thinkers. We encourage children to think, reason, question, experiment, make choices, and develop self-help and practical skills. Our beautiful environment includes age-appropriate play areas for children. We served the children Organic, nutritious meals and snacks, made fresh on premise daily.

Euredice Nunez


My name is Euredice Nunez, I am the owner and child care provider of Lollipop Early Learning since 2002. Currently, I am an accredited child care provider by the National Accreditation for Family Child Care (NAFCC). I also have a Child Development Associate “CDA” bilingual certificate awarded by the council of professional Recognition. I’m a member of the National Association for Family child Care (NAFCC) and a member of the United Federation of Teachers (UFT) I love to see the children laugh, learn and grow brings me much joy! I believe all children deserve to have a safe, educational environment to play, learn and grow. I strive to provide the highest quality of child care possible and lasting education in each child that comes to Lollipop Early Learning.
